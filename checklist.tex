\section*{NeurIPS Paper Checklist}
\begin{enumerate}
\item {\bf Claims}
\item[] Question: Do the main claims made in the abstract and introduction accurately reflect the paper's contributions and scope?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: The abstract and introduction state the natural-language elicitation, posterior-aware mechanism design, and DSIC/IR-with-respect-to-elicited-model scope, consistent with Sections 4--8.

\item {\bf Limitations}
\item[] Question: Does the paper discuss the limitations of the work performed by the authors?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Section 9 discusses external validity limits, elicitation error, and deployment cautions.

\item {\bf Theory assumptions and proofs}
\item[] Question: For each theoretical result, does the paper provide the full set of assumptions and a complete (and correct) proof?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Assumptions and proof sketches are given for DSIC, IR, and regret bound in Section 6.

\item {\bf Experimental result reproducibility}
\item[] Question: Does the paper fully disclose all the information needed to reproduce the main experimental results of the paper to the extent that it affects the main claims and/or conclusions of the paper?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Main settings, metrics, perturbations, and appendix hyperparameters are provided in Sections 7--8 and Appendix A--C.

\item {\bf Open access to data and code}
\item[] Question: Does the paper provide open access to the data and code, with sufficient instructions to faithfully reproduce the main experimental results?
\item[] Answer: \answerNo{}.
\item[] Justification: This submission provides paper artifacts and benchmark descriptions but not a public code release link.

\item {\bf Experimental setting/details}
\item[] Question: Does the paper specify all the training and test details necessary to understand the results?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Table A1 and Sections 7--8 provide training splits, optimization settings, and evaluation protocol.

\item {\bf Experiment statistical significance}
\item[] Question: Does the paper report error bars suitably and correctly defined or other appropriate information about the statistical significance?
\item[] Answer: \answerNo{}.
\item[] Justification: The current artifact reports representative aggregate values; formal confidence intervals are deferred.

\item {\bf Experiments compute resources}
\item[] Question: For each experiment, does the paper provide sufficient information on the computer resources needed to reproduce the experiments?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Table A1 reports hardware class and major training configuration.

\item {\bf Code of ethics}
\item[] Question: Does the research conform with the NeurIPS Code of Ethics?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: The study uses synthetic benchmark-derived data and reports limitations and responsible-use cautions.

\item {\bf Broader impacts}
\item[] Question: Does the paper discuss both potential positive and negative societal impacts?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Section 9 includes potential efficiency gains and risks from language/model bias and mis-elicitation.

\item {\bf Safeguards}
\item[] Question: Does the paper describe safeguards for responsible release of high-risk assets?
\item[] Answer: \answerNA{}.
\item[] Justification: No high-risk new model or dataset asset is released with this manuscript.

\item {\bf Licenses for existing assets}
\item[] Question: Are creators of existing assets credited and terms of use respected?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: Prior datasets and methods are cited throughout Related Work and references.

\item {\bf New assets}
\item[] Question: Are new assets introduced in the paper well documented?
\item[] Answer: \answerNo{}.
\item[] Justification: NL-CATS construction is documented, but a standalone asset release package is not included.

\item {\bf Crowdsourcing and research with human subjects}
\item[] Question: For crowdsourcing experiments and research with human subjects, are instructions and compensation details provided?
\item[] Answer: \answerNA{}.
\item[] Justification: No human-subject data collection was conducted.

\item {\bf Institutional review board (IRB) approvals or equivalent for research with human subjects}
\item[] Question: Does the paper describe risks and IRB approvals for human subjects research?
\item[] Answer: \answerNA{}.
\item[] Justification: The work does not involve human-subject experiments.

\item {\bf Declaration of LLM usage}
\item[] Question: Does the paper describe usage of LLMs if it is an important component of the core methods?
\item[] Answer: \answerYes{}.
\item[] Justification: LLM elicitation is central and described in Sections 5, 7, and Appendix C.
\end{enumerate}
